Vijay

Vijay, also known as Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, is a prominent Indian actor, primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema. He is one of the highest-paid actors in India and enjoys a massive fan following, often referred to as "Thalapathy" by his fans. Beyond acting, Vijay has also ventured into politics, founding the Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam party in 2024.
